HRITIK DIXIT BIO


Hritik Dixit ( born 28 September 2004) is a Student who studies at Bhadohi, UP. He has portrayed a variety of characters and is known for his Study skills. He has won many awards, including Head Boy, Captain, Voice Captain, and one for Best Ancher of The St. Thomas School, Gyanpur. Started school in 2009.

The 2011 drawing competition for which Dixit won two best drawing Awards, was a turning point in his school life; After this in 2013 he also take participate in Speech and Debate. In 2015 he get the membership of school perimbles as the third member.

Dixit has also performed on stage. The latter, he became the Perfect of the school and he get the position of Vice Captain of the St Thomas School. Later he get several responsibilties like head of Assembly member , Monitor of the class etc.

He was born in Bhadohi, Uttar Pradesh to a family prominent in Goverment & Political. He is of Hindu and Brahmin descent on his paternal side. Hrithik's paternal grandmother Rama U Dixit was a Brahmin Hindu. His father, Political (Gram Pradhan) Vinay U Dixit, is the son of Block Development officer (B.D.O.) Udarnath R Dixit; his mother, Indu V Dixit, is the daughter of a Farmer Shreepati Mishra.

Hritik has two older sister, Shivani Pandey & Deepika Pandey, and two younger sisters Kavya and Ruhanika and was educated at the St. Thomas School. Although Dixit practices Hinduism, he says that "I'm not religious, I'm an INDIAN CITIZEN. I don't visit temples. But I hope there is a superpower."

Hritik has Three older Brothers, Akash, Vikas & Ankit and one Younger brother Daivik. All his those brothers are working with a Private Sector in Mumbai & Delhi. Also they are studing too.Hritik has one cute bhanji Mishka Pandey.
